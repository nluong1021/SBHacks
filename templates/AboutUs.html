About DARE

Welcome to  DARE ! Have you ever let your curiosity loose towards new languages or have you ever been insecure about not knowing being more cosmopolitan and worldly?
If you answered yes, then the DARE (Dictionary & Augmented Reality Explorer) is meant to be your companion! With the DARE pocket companion, you won't have to fret again over the meaning of billboards, packages, street names and signs in foreign languages. With DARE by your side, you will be able to translate instantly and accurately any foreign word you desire! you will be surprised at how easy it is to understand a language with our easy to use interface. Choose DARE to be your new friend and explore the world together!
